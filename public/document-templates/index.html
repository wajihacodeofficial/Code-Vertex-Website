1. Services & Engagement

The Agency agrees to provide specific software development and design services to the Client as defined in individual Statements of Work (SOW), which shall be governed by this Agreement. The Client agrees to provide all necessary credentials, feedback, and technical access within 3 business days of the Agency's request. Failure to do so may result in timeline suspension without liability to the Agency.

2. Payment Terms & IP Linkage

Fees for Services shall be outlined in the SOW. Intellectual Property (IP) rights to the final deliverables are transferred to the Client only upon full clearance of final payment. Until such time, the Agency retains full equitable and legal title to all work product. Unauthorized use of work product prior to final payment is a material breach of this Agreement.

3. Limitation of Liability

The Agency's total liability for any claim arising out of this Agreement is capped at the total value of the specific SOW from which the claim arose. Carve-outs: this cap does not apply to gross negligence, willful misconduct, third-party IP indemnification, or data breaches caused specifically by the Agency's proven negligence in adhering to defined security SOPs.

4. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by internet outages, third-party API failures (Stripe, AWS, OpenAI, etc.), natural disasters, or government restrictions. The Agency shall notify the Client within 48 hours of any Force Majeure event impacting project delivery.

5. Non-Solicitation

For 12 months following the completion of any Project, the Client shall not directly or indirectly hire, or refer for hire, any employee or contractor of the Agency without a referral fee equal to 100% of the individual's annual salary at the Agency.

6. Warranty Disclaimer

Except as stated in the PCC, Agency disclaims all implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for purpose. A 30-day bug-fix warranty is provided post-delivery for items explicitly listed in the SOW Acceptance Criteria.

7. Term & Termination

This Agreement may be terminated with 30 days written notice. Payment for work-in-progress is calculated at time-and-materials at the agreed hourly rate. Three late payments within any 6-month period constitute a material breach, granting the Agency immediate termination rights and suspension of all services.

8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Sindh, Pakistan. The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Karachi.
