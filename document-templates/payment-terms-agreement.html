1. Late Payments & Penalty

All invoices are due within 7 days. Overdue balances accrue 1.5% interest per month (subject to Sindh law). Production is suspended automatically 7 days after an overdue notice is issued.

2. Re-activation Fee

A mandatory $350 USD Re-activation Fee applies to restore a suspended project. Three or more instances of late payment constitute a material breach, triggering termination rights under the MSA.

3. Refund Policy

Deposits are non-refundable as they secure engineering capacity. Milestone payments are non-refundable once the milestone is signed off (MSO) per DOC-17.

4. Currency & Tax

All fees are in USD. The Client is responsible for all local taxes, withholding, or intermediary bank transfer fees to ensure the Agency receives the full invoiced amount.
