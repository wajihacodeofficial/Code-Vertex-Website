1. Executive Summary

Code Vertex is pleased to submit this proposal for the custom development of your restaurant and banquet digital platform. Our goal is to deliver a fast, reliable, and easy-to-manage website that elevates your brand, captures reservations directly, and gives your team full control over day-to-day content — with no technical knowledge required.

We will build your platform as a fully custom web application, purpose-built for your business. This means you get a tailored system — including your own booking engine and admin dashboard — that you fully own, without depending on expensive third-party subscriptions.

This proposal outlines the scope, timeline, and investment required to launch your platform within 5–6 weeks.

2. Strategic Business Objectives

  • Elevate Brand Authority: Engineer a premium digital presence that immediately separates you from local competitors.
  • Zero-Commission Independence: Deploy a custom booking engine to capture reservations directly, bypassing third-party SaaS fees.
  • Maximum Conversion: Guarantee sub-second mobile performance to prevent user drop-off and maximize table turn-over.
  • Operational Simplicity: Deliver a secure administrative dashboard customized strictly for your staff's workflow.
  • Infinite Scalability: Architect the platform for modular expansion as you open new locations or scale your catering operations.

3. Scope of Work

Pages to be Developed:

Home | About Us | Menu | Banquet Facility | Gallery | Catering | Contact Us | Visiting Hours | Online Booking | Menu Kit

Key Features & Deliverables:

  • Direct Reservation Engine: A simple, clean booking form allowing guests to submit reservation requests directly. All inquiries are automatically emailed to your team and saved to a secure management dashboard (Note: Live table inventory and online payments are scoped for Phase 2).
  • Easy-to-Use Admin Dashboard: A private, password-protected control panel where your staff can update the Menu PDF, Gallery photos, visiting hours, and page content — no technical knowledge required. Full control without developer dependency.
  • Performance & SEO: Fast-loading on all devices with basic SEO setup included. Existing Google rankings are protected through proper redirect handling during migration.
  • Integrations Included: WhatsApp contact button, Google Maps embed, and mobile-optimized layout — all included at no extra cost.

4. Technical Approach & Why a Custom Build

Your platform will be built as a fully custom web application — not assembled from generic templates or third-party plugins. This gives you a faster, more secure, and easier-to-manage product that is tailored exactly to your workflow.

Factor Custom-Built Platform (Our Approach) Generic Template Solutions
Ownership You own 100% of the code. No recurring subscription fees. Dependent on third-party platforms and monthly billing cycles.
Speed Optimized for fast loading on mobile — directly improves reservations. Template bloat slows page load, increasing bounce rates.
Admin Ease A simple dashboard built only for your content — no clutter. Generic admin panels with unnecessary options and steep learning curves.
Security Custom architecture with no publicly-known attack surface. Standard platforms are frequent targets for automated attacks.

Hosting & Deployment

  • Your website will be deployed on a reliable cloud hosting platform (e.g., Vercel, DigitalOcean, or Render).
  • Estimated hosting cost: $150–$250/year, billed directly to your own hosting account.
  • Full server configuration, SSL certificate, and deployment are handled by us — no technical setup required on your end.
