1. Executive Summary

This proposal covers a fully custom-built digital platform for your restaurant and banquet venue — not a template, not a plugin stack. Specifically: a 10-page mobile-optimized website, a database-backed reservation system that emails your team instantly and logs every request, and a private admin dashboard your staff can operate after a single 30-minute walkthrough.

You own the code outright. No monthly platform fees. No third-party booking service taking a cut of your reservations. The $1,600 covers design, development, testing, and live deployment — one fixed price, nothing hidden.

2. Why This Investment Pays for Itself

  • Eliminates Third-Party Booking Fees: Platforms like OpenTable and SevenRooms charge $150–$350/month. This custom booking engine does the same job — owned by you, no recurring fees. The $1,600 investment pays for itself in under 6 months. After that, you run on ~$150–$250/year in hosting.
  • Direct Reservations, Zero Middlemen: Every booking goes straight to your inbox and your admin dashboard. No commission per cover. No competitor ads on your booking page. You own the customer relationship entirely.
  • Mobile Performance = Revenue: Over 70% of restaurant searches happen on mobile. A slow or generic site loses the customer before they see your menu. This platform is engineered specifically for sub-second mobile load times.
  • Your Team Stays Independent: No developer needed to update a menu, swap a photo, or change your hours. Your staff can do it in under 2 minutes from any device.
  • Expandable Without Rebuilding: Stripe payments, live table inventory, loyalty programs — all scoped for Phase 2 and addable cleanly without touching the core system.
